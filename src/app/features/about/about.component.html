About ProTectVinyl

Cabinet protection that respects your cabinets

ProTectVinyl makes paintable, removable barrier strips for the base of floor-length cabinets — the strike zone where wheelchair footrests, walkers, and everyday life chip and scuff doors first.

Our Story

Started in an accessible kitchen remodel

ProTectVinyl began on an accessible kitchen remodel, when a cabinet installer noticed the same damage in every wheelchair user's home: floor-length cabinet doors chipped and scuffed along the bottom few inches, exactly where footrests and casters strike. Touch-up kits never matched, refinishing cost more than the doors, and nothing on the market could be painted to blend in. So we engineered a 20-mil vinyl barrier strip with a factory-primed surface that takes paint like trim — and peels away clean when you want it gone.

Today we cut barrier strips for kitchens in 48 states: standard sizes matched to the base widths of the cabinet lines national suppliers ship everywhere, and custom cuts for the one-of-a-kind cabinets local shops build. Every order — one base panel or a whole renovation — is cut to within 1/16 of an inch.
