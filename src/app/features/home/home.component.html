Custom Cabinets Welcome

Custom measurements for custom cabinets

Locally built, refaced, or heirloom cabinetry rarely matches a standard size chart — so we cut to your numbers instead. Every custom order is cut to within 1/16 of an inch of the measurements you provide.

Measure the width of each cabinet run and how high the strike zone reaches — a wheelchair footrest usually lands in the first 8 inches above the floor. No minimum order, whether you need one base panel or an entire accessible kitchen.

Start a Custom Order

Measuring checklist

  • Width: measure along the bottom edge of the cabinet run, end to end.
  • Height: measure up from the floor to just above the highest scuff or strike point — typically 4"–8" for wheelchair footrests.
  • Profile: note flat-panel, shaker, or raised-panel doors so we match the coverage area.
  • Precision: round to the nearest 1/16 of an inch; we cut to that number.
